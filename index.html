Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He was one of India's greatest scientists and served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.

He played a major role in India's missile and space programmes, earning him the title "Missile Man of India."

Dr. Kalam received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award. He inspired millions of students with his books, speeches and vision for a developed India.

His famous books include Wings of Fire, Ignited Minds, and India 2020.
